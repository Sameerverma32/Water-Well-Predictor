Process

How WellSense Works

Four intelligent steps from your inputs to a precise well prediction in seconds.

STEP — 01
📍
Enter Location & Soil Data

Provide your district, state, soil type, annual rainfall, topography, and nearby land-use information. The system auto-populates known geological parameters.

STEP — 02
⚙️
ML Model Processes Inputs

Your data is normalized, encoded, and passed through our trained Random Forest model — built on thousands of verified well records from CGWB and state boards.

STEP — 03
💧
Aquifer Zone Identified

The model predicts the depth to water table, identifies the aquifer zone (phreatic or confined), and estimates seasonal fluctuation ranges for the location.

STEP — 04
📋
Report & Recommendations

Receive a full prediction report with optimal well depth, casing depth, expected yield in LPH, drilling cost estimate, and sustainability classification.

Capabilities

Everything You Need
for Well Planning

🗺️
District-wise Predictions

Covers 700+ districts across 36 Indian states and UTs with region-specific geological training data for highly localized predictions.

📉
Water Table Depth Forecast

Predict pre-monsoon and post-monsoon water table depths with seasonal variation modelling based on 20+ years of historical data.

🧪
Soil & Geology Analysis

Input soil texture, permeability, lithology, and rock type — the model understands how subsurface composition affects aquifer depth and yield.

⚖️
Multi-Model Comparison

8 ML algorithms benchmarked. Random Forest selected as champion with 95% R² — results also show confidence intervals from ensemble predictions.

🔐
Secure & Role-Based Access

JWT authentication with separate portals for government water boards, engineers, researchers, and farmers — each with appropriate data access levels.

📄
Downloadable PDF Reports

Generate professional well planning reports with geological cross-section diagrams, depth recommendations, cost estimates, and sustainability ratings.
